Nemo , the winer of Eurovision song contest 2024

At the international song contest "Eurovision 2024", the final of which was held on May 11, representatives of Switzerland Nemo won. Behind this pseudonym hides the performer Nemo Mettler, who identifies himself as a non-binary person, which is why he asks to use the pronoun "they".
